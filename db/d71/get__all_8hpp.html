void engine::GetAll (std::vector< TaskWithResult< void >> &tasks)
 Waits for the successful completion of all of the specified tasks or the cancellation of the caller. Effectively performs for (auto& task : tasks) task.Get(); with a twist: task.Get() is called in tasks completion order rather than in provided order, thus exceptions are rethrown ASAP.
